Color Variant Generator

Welcome to the Color Variant Generator! Rather than being a tool for creating color palettes, this is meant as a tool for creating slight or great variants on existing colors. To use, enter in your original colors and then specify how much variance you want. Values like 10 or 20 creates slight variants on the original color while values like 70 to 100 will greatly change the color. Feel free to experiment and I hope you enjoy.
